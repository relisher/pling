penn undergraduate linguistics society

Pliŋ is Penn’s Linguistics student organization. We look to build a community within linguistics, and we work to provide students with as many opportunities as we can. Whether we're planning trips to conferences, speaker series, or dinner get-togethers, we're always looking to connect the students and to promote the department.
